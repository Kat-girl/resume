Transcript

- I want to ask you some very random questions if you don't mind because I, you know, I think you're an interesting guy, I'm interested in you. Have you ever met another Keanu?

- Yes, I have a cousin...

- And nobody said that we got a Keanu already, maybe we should just stick with the one?

- Yeah but I didn't meet that guy until I was probably eight or nine oh I had never heard that name for another human until that moment. And I was just like wow, ok, Keanu. I've never met a Keanu before.

- Do you have a nickname or have you ever had a nickname?

- I have yeah.

- Can you share that?

- You know, people call me different things sometimes. You know, well, that's really, that's fun, my middle name is Charles, so I have this one friend once calls me Charlie.

- Charlie?

- Yeah, okay, all right.

Have you ever seen a ghost?

- Yes.

- You have?!

- Yeah, when I was a kid.

- In Hawaii?

- No, it was in New York. It was cool.

- What happened?

- Okay, okay, so, I'm like a little kid, I'm probably like six or seven years old, and our new apartment, and we just come from probably Australia. Anyway so Ranade, nanny, in the bedroom, she's sitting my sisters asleep, she's sitting over there, there's a doorway. All of a sudden we're looking over there, and this like jacket comes waving through the doorway, and empty, there's no head, there's no body, there's no legs, it's just there! And then it disappears, and I was a little kid and I thought: okay well, that's interesting. And then I looked over at the nanny and she's like this, and I'm like oh wow so that was real!

- Wow, cool!

- Is that a ghost or is that just some weird floating jacket?

- That's a reason to move to me

Do you still have your tonsils?

- Yes

- Okay, both of them?

- Sure.

- What movie of all the movies you made was the least fun to make?

- Come on Jimmy, I can't tell.

- What was the most fun to make?

- Ah, you know, I've had the opportunity to work on a few projects that have changed my life, as an artist and as a person, and today I'm going to choose the Matrix trilogy!
